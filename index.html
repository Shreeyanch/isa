Club Objectives

This objective and aim for this club can be expressed as:

- TO BRING TOGETHER STUDENTS FROM ALL TYPES OF BACKGROUNDS.

- HELP TO SETTLE AS A INTERNATIONAL STUDENT/ BUILD A HOME AWAY FROM HOME

- TO BRING THE DIVERSITY TO THE CAMPUS

- OPPORTUNITIES FOR STUDENTS TO EXPRESS THEIR IDENTITIES, CULTURES, TRADITION AND LEARN ABOUT OTHERS

- TO SERVE AS A DIRECT MEDIUM OF COMMUNICATION TO INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

- TO CELEBRATE AND SIGNIFY EVERY POSSIBLE CULTURE.

- TO BE RECOGNIZED AS A TRUSTY BODY FOR FOREIGN AFFAIRS.

- TO SOLVE OVERALL PROBLEMS LIKE FEES, STUDY, HOMESICKNESS ETC
